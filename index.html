Pleasure without performance.

So much of what women learn about their bodies is shaped by performance and expectation — Inner Tide exists to unlearn that. Tantra, at its root, is a way of paying attention: to breath, to sensation, to what feels true in the moment.

I.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions, answered plainly.

Is this an erotic massage or escort service?

No. Inner Tide is a private tantric bodywork practice. Our focus is your relationship with your own body — presence, sensation, safety, and self-connection — not performance or entertainment. Sessions are conducted with the professionalism and structure of a wellness practice.

What should I expect, step by step?

You reach out through the private booking form or by email, sharing a little about your intentions. Nothing about the correspondence is branded or identifiable. When you arrive, we begin with an unhurried conversation about your comfort and what you're hoping to explore, then settle into grounding together before any touch begins. The session itself unfolds at your pace, guided by breath and ongoing communication. Afterward, there's quiet time to rest and return to yourself, with space for a short conversation about anything that came up — only if you'd like one.

Do I have to include breast or yoni massage?

Never. A complete, deeply nurturing session can consist entirely of full-body massage. Any more intimate element is entirely optional, discussed in advance, and only ever included with your clear, explicit consent.

What if I've never tried tantra before?

Most clients haven't. No experience or flexibility is required — only a willingness to be present. We'll explain everything as we go, and you can ask questions at any point.

Is this only for a certain body type, age, or orientation?

No. Inner Tide welcomes women of every age, body type, sexual orientation, and relationship status. This practice is built on the belief that every woman deserves attentive, respectful touch.

Where do sessions take place?

Either privately in your own home, anywhere across Montreal, Laval, Longueuil, Brossard, and the surrounding area, or in our discreet private studio. Location details for the studio are shared only after intake, for your privacy.

How is my privacy protected?

All correspondence is confidential and unbranded. Intake information is never shared. Studio visits require no sign-in or shared waiting areas, and in-home visits are conducted with complete discretion, including unmarked arrival.

What should I do to prepare?

Arrive freshly showered if possible, avoid a heavy meal beforehand, and wear something comfortable to travel in. Beyond that, there's nothing to prepare — this is a space to simply arrive.

Can I choose what happens during the session?

Yes. Every session is tailored to your comfort, intentions, and preferences. You're always welcome to ask questions, pause, or change your mind at any point.

Is this practice legal and above board?

Inner Tide operates as a bodywork and embodiment practice grounded in professional, consent-based touch, not sexual services for hire. We take our legal and ethical obligations seriously and structure every session accordingly.

Do you offer sessions for couples?

Couples' sessions focused on teaching partners mindful, connective touch are launching soon. Join our list via the contact page to be notified first.
